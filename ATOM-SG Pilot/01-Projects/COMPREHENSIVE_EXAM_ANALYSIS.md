## Analysis Categories

### Category 1: Geometry Questions
**Sub-categories:**
- G1: Angle Reasoning (protractor, angles at point, vertically opposite, triangle angles)
- G2: Area & Perimeter (composite shapes, L-shapes, cut-outs)
- G3: Volume & 3D (cuboids, solid views)
- G4: Properties (rhombus, trapezium, parallelogram properties)
- G5: Composite Overlap (overlapping shapes)
- G6: Grid Construction (1cm grid, protractor overlay)
- G7: 3D Visualization (isometric views, orthographic projections)
- G8: Angle Chasing (complex angle relationships)

### Category 2: Data Interpretation
**Sub-categories:**
- DI1: Line Graphs (rate of change, reverse percentage)
- DI2: Bar Graphs/Charts (comparison, totals)
- DI3: Pie Charts (percentages, fractions)
- DI4: Tables (data extraction, calculations)

### Category 3: Word Problems
**Sub-categories:**
- WP1: Before-After Change
- WP2: Part-Whole Comparison
- WP3: Cross-Thread Collision
- WP4: Excess-Shortage
- WP5: Rate/Distance/Time
