notes: |
  Easy-level problem testing bar graph interpretation with total and average calculation.
  Students must read all values, sum them, and calculate the mean.

  Solution reasoning:
  Step 1 - Read all values from the bar graph:
  - January: 80 mm
  - February: 60 mm
  - March: 120 mm
  - April: 100 mm
  - May: 140 mm
  - June: 160 mm

  Step 2 - Calculate total rainfall:
  - Total = 80 + 60 + 120 + 100 + 140 + 160
  - Total = 660 mm

  Step 3 - Calculate average monthly rainfall:
  - Average = Total ÷ Number of months
  - Average = 660 ÷ 6
  - Average = 110 mm

  Common errors:
  - Misreading bar heights (e.g., reading 140 as 120)
  - Forgetting to include all 6 months in the total
  - Dividing by wrong number for average

  Recognition skill taught: To find average from a bar graph: (1) read all values, (2) calculate total, (3) divide by number of categories.
