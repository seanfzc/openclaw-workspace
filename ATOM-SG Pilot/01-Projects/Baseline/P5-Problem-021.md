notes: |
  Easy-level problem testing basic bar graph reading skills.
  Students must read values directly from the bars and perform simple comparison.

  Solution reasoning:
  Question 1: "How many students chose Orange?"
  1. Locate the bar labeled "Orange" on the x-axis
  2. Read the height of the bar against the y-axis scale
  3. The bar reaches the line marked 15
  4. Answer: 15 students

  Question 2: "How many more students chose Apple than Grapes?"
  1. Find the height of the Apple bar: 12 students
  2. Find the height of the Grapes bar: 6 students
  3. Subtract: 12 - 6 = 6
  4. Answer: 6 more students chose Apple than Grapes

  Recognition skill taught: Bar graphs show quantities as bar heights; read the scale carefully and compare by subtraction.
