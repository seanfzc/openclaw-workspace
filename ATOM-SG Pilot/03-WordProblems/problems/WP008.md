pathwayType: Part-Whole with Comparison
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: A is m/n of B, B is p/q of C, express A as fraction of C, find relationship
traps: Students chain multiply fractions incorrectly; lose track of which quantity is the reference whole
question: John has 3/5 as many stamps as Mary. Mary has 5/6 as many stamps as Tom. If Tom has 72 stamps, how many stamps does John have?
answer: 36 stamps
solutionSteps:
  - Mary = 5/6 × Tom = 5/6 × 72 = 60 stamps
  - John = 3/5 × Mary = 3/5 × 60 = 36 stamps
  - Or: John = 3/5 × 5/6 × Tom = 3/6 × 72 = 36 stamps
