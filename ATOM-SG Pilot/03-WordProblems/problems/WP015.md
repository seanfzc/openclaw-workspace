type: problem
pathwayType: Part-Whole with Comparison
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: Difference between two parts = Known value, express both in terms of units, solve
traps: Students add instead of subtract; confuse which quantity is larger
question: Jenny and Kelly have 84 stickers altogether. Jenny has 16 more stickers than Kelly. How many stickers does Kelly have?
answer: 34 stickers
solutionSteps:
  - Let Kelly = x, then Jenny = x + 16
  - Total: x + (x + 16) = 84
  - 2x + 16 = 84
  - 2x = 68
  - x = 34
