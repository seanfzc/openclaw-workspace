pathwayType: Part-Whole with Comparison
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: Given ratio and difference between parts, find actual values
traps: Students confuse difference with total; divide difference by wrong number of parts
question: The ratio of boys to girls in a club is 5:3. There are 24 more boys than girls. How many children are in the club?
answer: 96 children
solutionSteps:
  - Difference in ratio: 5 - 3 = 2 parts
  - 2 parts = 24 children
  - 1 part = 12 children
  - Total parts = 5 + 3 = 8 parts
  - Total children = 8 × 12 = 96 children
