type: problem
pathwayType: Before-After Change
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: Start with some, add fraction of current, result known, find start
traps: Students add fraction to original instead of calculating fraction of current amount
question: Mrs. Tan had some money in her purse. She spent $25 on groceries and then deposited 1/4 of the remainder into her bank account. If she had $45 left, how much did she have at first?
answer: $85
solutionSteps:
  - After spending $25: Remainder = Original - 25
  - Deposited 1/4 of remainder, so 3/4 of remainder = $45
  - Remainder = $45 ÷ (3/4) = $45 × 4/3 = $60
  - Original = $60 + $25 = $85
articulationRubric:
  level1: "Mentions spending and depositing money"
  level2: "Identifies two changes, attempts to work backwards"
  level3: "Articulates: Before-After, spend $25 first, then 1/4 of remainder deposited (3/4 left = $45), work backwards to find original"
