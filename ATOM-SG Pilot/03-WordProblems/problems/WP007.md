pathwayType: Before-After Change
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: Original × (1 - first fraction) × (1 - second fraction) = Final, find original from final
traps: Students calculate fractions sequentially forward; struggle to reverse the operations
question: A tank was full of water. After 3/7 of the water was poured out, then 2/5 of the remaining water was used, 48 litres were left. How much water was in the tank at first?
answer: 140 litres
solutionSteps:
  - After first pour: 1 - 3/7 = 4/7 remains
  - After second use: 4/7 × (1 - 2/5) = 4/7 × 3/5 = 12/35 remains
  - 12/35 of original = 48 litres
  - Original = 48 × 35/12 = 140 litres
