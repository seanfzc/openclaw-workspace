problemID: WP012
type: problem
pathwayType: Before-After Change
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: Initial + Addition - Removal = Final, track inventory changes sequentially
traps: Students confuse addition and subtraction; apply changes to wrong base amount
question: A library had 450 books. They received 120 new books on Monday. On Tuesday, they donated 1/5 of their total books to a school. How many books did they have left?
answer: 456 books
solutionSteps:
  - After Monday: 450 + 120 = 570 books
  - Tuesday donation: 570 × 1/5 = 114 books
  - Left: 570 - 114 = 456 books
articulationRubric:
  level1: "Mentions books received and donated"
  level2: "Identifies sequential changes, calculates each step"
  level3: "Articulates: Before-After Change, start 450, add 120, then remove 1/5 of new total (not original), final = 456"
