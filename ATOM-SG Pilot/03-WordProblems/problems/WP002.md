type: problem
pathwayType: Before-After Change
difficulty: Medium
equationShadow: After first change, then second change from remainder, final amount known, find original by reversing both changes
traps: Students calculate 1/4 of original instead of 1/4 of remainder; forget to reverse operations in correct order
question: Sarah spent 3/8 of her money on a book and 2/5 of the remainder on a pen. If she had $30 left, how much money did she have at first?
answer: $80
solutionSteps:
  - After book: Remainder = 1 - 3/8 = 5/8
  - After pen: Remainder = 1 - 2/5 = 3/5 of previous
  - Final remainder = 5/8 × 3/5 = 3/8 of original
  - Original = $30 ÷ (3/8) = $30 × 8/3 = $80

  level1: "Mentions spending money on book and pen"
  level2: "Describes two purchases with fractions, knows to work backwards"
  level3: "Articulates: 3/8 spent first, then 2/5 of remainder, $30 left is 3/8 of original, reverse to find original"
