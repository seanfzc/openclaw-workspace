# Squirrel AI
*Type: organization*

## Overview
Squirrel AI is an adaptive learning platform that uses artificial intelligence to diagnose student knowledge gaps and deliver personalized learning paths. Founded in China, it serves millions of students and represents one of the most advanced implementations of AI in education, demonstrating 34% improvement in learning efficiency compared to traditional classrooms.
