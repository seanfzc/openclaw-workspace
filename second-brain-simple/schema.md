## 🎯 Mission Statement
This schema defines how your external brain organizes knowledge. It's tailored to support:
1. **Energy sector leadership** (Aurora Energy Research)
2. **Fintech/AI entrepreneurship** (Two Alpha Group)
3. **Blockchain community building** (Chainlink)
4. **Education innovation** for your sons' future (11 & 6 years old) - PRIMARY FOCUS
5. **Operational excellence** across APAC markets
6. **Strategic decision-making** with AI-native perspective

The unique lens: **Everything connects back to preparing your boys for their future through game-based learning systems**

## 🎮 Education Vision - CORE MISSION
Transform ALL your professional knowledge into immersive game-based learning systems for your sons (11 & 6) that:
1. **Address learning gaps** like Squirrel AI - using AI to diagnose and fill specific knowledge/skill gaps
2. **Develop critical thinking & leadership** through age-appropriate challenges and decision-making games
3. **Leverage psychology & communication** - understanding human behavior to design engaging learning experiences
4. **Prepare for their future** based on what we know today about emerging technologies, economies, and social systems
5. **Make learning invisible** - wrap everything in games so they learn without realizing it (ultimate goal)
6. **Build future-proof skills** - focus on adaptability, problem-solving, emotional intelligence, and systems thinking
7. **Connect all domains** - show how energy, fintech, blockchain, and AI intersect in the real world through play

**Philosophy:** Every professional insight, every industry trend, every strategic decision can be transformed into a learning experience that prepares your boys for a world we can't yet fully imagine.

### **Auto-Linking Rules**
1. **Entity Recognition**: Auto-detect and link to existing entity pages
2. **Concept Mapping**: Connect mentions to concept definitions
3. **Bi-Directional**: Links work both ways (A→B creates B→A)
4. **Contextual Notes**: Explain *why* things are connected

### **Linking Conventions**
- Use `[[entity-name]]` for wiki pages
- Use `[display text](https://...)` for external references
- Create backlinks automatically
- Maintain link consistency across all pages

### **Connection Types**
- `relates-to:` General association
- `influences:` Causal relationship
- `contradicts:` Opposite viewpoints
- `builds-on:` Extends or develops
- `alternatives:` Competing approaches

## 🎨 Brand Voice & Style Guide
# How the LLM should write and organize content

### **Tone & Voice**
- **Primary:** Strategic, technical, authoritative
- **Secondary:** Warm, community-focused for volunteer content
- **Avoid:** Corporate buzzwords, em dashes (—), passive voice
- **Embrace:** Clear headings, bulleted lists, ROI-focused language

### **Key Phrases to Use**
- Lead generation, ROI, risk management, digital transformation
- "Eating bitter" (enduring hardship/stoicism)
- Operational excellence, strategic alignment, value proposition
- Data-driven decisions, evidence-based approaches

### **Structure Preferences**
- Executive summary first, details after
- Bulleted lists for key insights
- Clear section headers (##, ###)
- Tables for comparisons when helpful
- Code blocks for technical content

### **Quality Standards**
1. **Clarity:** Easy to scan in 60 seconds
2. **Actionability:** Clear next steps or implications
3. **Connections:** Explicit links to related knowledge
4. **Metadata:** Complete and accurate
5. **Source Integrity:** Faithful to original material

## 🔄 Processing Workflow
# How raw inputs become organized knowledge

### **Ingestion Pipeline**
```
Raw File → LLM Reads → Extract Insights → Create Pages → Update Index → Log Activity
    ↓           ↓           ↓             ↓            ↓           ↓
  .pdf/.md    Content    Entities       Sources     Catalog    History
              Analysis   Concepts       Entities    Update     Record
                        Connections    Concepts
```

### **Quality Gates**
1. **Completeness Check:** All metadata fields populated
2. **Connection Audit:** Minimum 3 relevant links established
3. **Clarity Review:** Executive summary passes "60-second test"
4. **Actionability Score:** At least 2 actionable insights identified
5. **Confidence Calibration:** Appropriate confidence level assigned

### **Automation Rules**
- New files in `raw/` trigger auto-ingestion (optional)
- Weekly cross-linking maintenance
- Monthly schema compliance checks
- Quarterly knowledge gap analysis
